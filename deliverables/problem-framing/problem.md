## Problem: Communities 

In many neighborhoods and communities, people will often buy tools or supplies, like a specific screwdriver or an exact shade of blue yarn, that they only use once or twice, while others nearby might already have what they need but have no easy way to share it. Renting from stores can be expensive and sometimes buying something in a small quantity is impossible. Sharing these items on social media, community pages, or even Craigslist is often unreliable and unorganized and it leads to a lot of useful items sitting unused.
